Timeline of the story
Catherine’s Departure to Bath

Catherine Morland is invited by the Allens to Bath for the season, excited about the adventure ahead.

Meeting Henry Tilney

Catherine meets Henry Tilney at a ball and is immediately struck by his charm and wit.

Friendship with Isabella Thorpe

Catherine strikes up a close friendship with Isabella Thorpe, who becomes involved with her brother James.

Catherine’s First Dance with Henry

Catherine dances with Henry for the first time, deepening their connection.

John Thorpe’s Pursuit of Catherine

John Thorpe tries to woo Catherine, but she finds him overbearing and unappealing.

The Engagement of Isabella and James

Isabella and James become engaged, but Isabella’s affection for James starts to wane as she grows attracted to Captain Tilney.

Catherine’s Invitation to Northanger Abbey

Catherine receives an invitation from Eleanor Tilney to visit Northanger Abbey, a prospect she is eager to explore.

Catherine's Imaginations Run Wild

Upon arriving at Northanger Abbey, Catherine’s imagination leads her to believe in dark secrets and a mysterious past.

Isabella’s Betrayal

Isabella breaks off her engagement with James, setting her sights on Captain Tilney instead.

Catherine’s Sudden Dismissal from Northanger Abbey

General Tilney expels Catherine from Northanger Abbey when he learns she is not wealthy, crushing her hopes.

Catherine’s Return Home

Catherine returns home, heartbroken and confused by General Tilney's rejection.

Henry’s Proposal

Henry Tilney visits Catherine at her home and proposes marriage, revealing his true feelings for her despite his father’s objections.

Catherine and Henry’s Marriage

The novel ends with Catherine and Henry happily engaged, showing love built on sincerity and mutual respect.
