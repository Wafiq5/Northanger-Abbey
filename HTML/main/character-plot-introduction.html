Know about the characters!
Morlands
Catherine Morland
Catherine Morland is a bright, imaginative, and kind-hearted young woman. She has a love for Gothic novels, which fuels her vivid imagination. Though inexperienced in society, she is genuine, curious, and eager to see the world beyond her quiet upbringing.
Mrs. Morland
Mrs. Morland, Catherine’s mother, is a practical, sensible, and caring woman. She provides steady guidance to her children but is not overly sentimental. She values common sense and does not indulge in excessive worries or dramatic notions.
Mr. Morland
Mr. Morland is a kind and sensible clergyman who provides a modest but comfortable life for his family. He is practical, good-natured, and not deeply involved in the novel’s events.
James Morland
James Morland is a friendly and enthusiastic young man. He's Catherine Morland's brother and a close friend of John Thorpe. He's often portrayed as somewhat naive and easily influenced, particularly by his friend, John.
Tilney
Henry Tilney
Henry Tilney is charming, witty, and intelligent. He has a playful sense of humor and a keen understanding of human nature. As a well-read clergyman, he balances sarcasm with kindness, making him both perceptive and approachable.
Captain Tilney
Captain Tilney's known for being very charming and polite when you first meet him. But, as you get to know him better, you realize he's not as nice as he seemed at the beginning.
General Tilney
General Tilney is a character who's the father of Henry and Captain Tilney. He's a powerful and wealthy man, and he can be quite imposing. He's very concerned with appearances and social standing, and he likes things done his way.
Elenor Tilney
Eleanor Tilney is a kind and gentle character. She's Henry Tilney's sister and is known for being thoughtful and intelligent. She's a good friend to Catherine and is generally seen as very sensible and caring.
Allen
Mr Allen
Mr. Allen is a kind and well-off gentleman. He's like a friendly uncle figure to Catherine, and he and his wife take her to Bath. He's a quiet and steady person, and mostly enjoys his card games!
Mrs Allen
Mrs. Allen is a sweet and friendly lady who's very kind to Catherine. She's also really interested in fashion and loves to admire and talk about pretty dresses and outfits! She's very nice, and takes Catherine to Bath.
Thorpe
John Thorpe
He's a very confident, some might say too confident, young man. He likes to brag and can be a bit pushy. He's friends with James Morland, and he's very interested in things like horses and carriages.
Isabella Thorpe
She's a very lively and fashionable young woman, and she's very good at making friends. She can also be a bit changeable in her feelings.
Mrs. Thorpe
She's John and Isabella's mother. She seems to let her children do pretty much what they want.
